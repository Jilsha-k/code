DELHI


Delhi,[a] officially the National Capital Territory (NCT) of Delhi, is a city and a union territory of India containing New Delhi, the capital of India.The city is popular for its enriched cultureandheritage.The city is the famous historical monuments and is developing with the passing of time.Delhi is of great historical significance as an important commercial, transport, and cultural hub, as well as the political centre of India.Delhi, city and national capital territory, north-central India. The city of Delhi actually consists of two components: Old Delhi, in the north, the historic city; and New Delhi, in the south, since 1947 the capital of India, built in the first part of the 20th century as the capital of British India.New delhi is the capital of Delhi.It is regarded as the heart of the nation is not just the capital but the heart and soul of Indian civilization.
